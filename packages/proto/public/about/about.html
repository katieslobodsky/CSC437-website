ABOUT

We’re the Cal Poly Hiking and Backpacking Club. We host a weekly hike in San Luis Obispo County every week, and we organize a couple of backpacking trips each quarter to amazing places across California, from the coast to the Sierra and beyond. All levels are welcome.

Tips for hikes and trips

  • Be prepared

    Bring water, snacks, layers, sun protection, and sturdy shoes.

  • Buddy system

    Stay with the group. Speak up if you need a pace or water break.

  • Leave No Trace

    Pack out all trash, stick to trails, respect wildlife & other hikers.

  • Know your limits

    Choose mileage and elevation that match your experience and fitness.
