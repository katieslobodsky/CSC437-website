ABOUT

We’re the Cal Poly Hiking and Backpacking Club. We host a weekly hike in San Luis Obispo County every week, and we organize a couple of backpacking trips each quarter to amazing places across California, from the coast to the Sierra and beyond. All levels are welcome.

WHAT WE DO

Our weekly hikes explore local favorites around SLO County, like Bishop Peak, Montaña de Oro, and more, usually on weekends. Each quarter, we also plan 1–2 backpacking trips around California for hikers who want to spend a night (or a few) on trail.

Safety

  • Be prepared: bring water, snacks, layers, and appropriate footwear.
  • Buddy system: stay with the group; speak up if you need to stop.
  • Leave No Trace: pack out all trash and respect trails/wildlife.
  • Know your limits: choose hikes that match your level and experience.
